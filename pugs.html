🐕 The Essential Pug Facts

🐕

Snore Machines

Pugs are brachycephalic — their flat faces cause them to snore, snort, and wheeze constantly. This is a feature, not a bug. It's music.

🥰

Professional Nappers

Pugs sleep 14 hours a day on average. They have optimized their entire existence around rest. Respect the commitment.

🔥

Ancient Royalty

Bred by Chinese emperors and later adopted by European royalty. Pugs have always known they're royalty. They haven't forgotten.

🧡

A Grumble

🌞

📋 Pug Anatomy 101

The Face Wrinkles

Must be cleaned daily to prevent yeast/bacterial buildup. Use a soft damp cloth and dry thoroughly. The wrinkles are adorable and also a maintenance responsibility.

The Eyes

Large and prominent — prone to scratches and irritation. Watch for discharge, redness, or cloudiness. Pugs can get eye prolapse in extreme cases. Keep them clean.

The Snout

Flat (brachycephalic) means restricted airways. This is why pugs snore, overheat easily, and should not be over-exercised in heat. Keep them cool.

The Corkscrew Tail

Adorable but the base area can trap moisture and debris. Clean it regularly. Some pugs have tighter curls than others — monitor for skin irritation.

The Double Coat

Pugs shed an absurd amount for their size. Weekly brushing with a bristle brush dramatically reduces shedding. They shed year-round with extra intensity in spring/fall.

The Body

Pugs gain weight easily. Obesity severely worsens their breathing problems. Measured meals, no free-feeding, regular moderate walks. Keep them in healthy weight range.

❤ Pug Care Guide

🍴 Diet

  • 2 measured meals per day — no free feeding
  • Avoid: grapes, raisins, onions, chocolate, xylitol
  • Monitor weight closely — 14–18 lbs is healthy range
  • Adjust food with age and activity level

🏃 Exercise

  • Short walks — 20–30 min daily is plenty
  • NEVER walk in heat above 80°F / 27°C
  • Watch for heavy breathing, slowing, or sitting down
  • Swimming is excellent low-impact exercise

👲 Health Monitoring

  • Annual vet checkups minimum
  • Clean wrinkles and tail area weekly
  • Check eyes daily for discharge or redness
  • Watch for BOAS symptoms: excessive snoring, gagging

🧡 Mental Health

  • Pugs are extremely social — don't leave alone all day
  • They thrive with other pugs or dogs
  • Puzzle toys help keep their minds active
  • Belly rubs are a non-negotiable daily requirement
